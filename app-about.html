About us

Westerra Credit Union has a convenient location on Union, just south of Sixth Avenue in Lakewood. The Green Mountain branch offers full-service walk-up and drive-up ATM service 24 hours a day. We offer drive-up window service for credit union members. We are also a CO-OP Shared Branch location. We look forward to serving your banking needs while you are in Lakewood.
